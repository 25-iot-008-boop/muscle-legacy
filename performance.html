Camaro Performance

The Chevrolet Camaro is engineered for raw power, precision handling, and track-level performance.

Engine Power

Turbocharged I4, V6, and supercharged V8 options up to 650 HP.

Acceleration

0–100 km/h in ~4 seconds (ZL1).

Handling

Magnetic ride control for sharp cornering.

Aerodynamics

Designed for downforce and high-speed stability.

Braking

Brembo brakes for strong stopping power.

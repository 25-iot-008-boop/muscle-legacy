Camaro Performance

The Chevrolet Camaro is engineered for raw power, precision handling, and track-level performance. Built with aggressive aerodynamics and high-performance engineering.

Engine Power

Turbocharged I4, V6, and supercharged V8 options delivering up to 650 HP.

Acceleration

0–100 km/h in approximately 4 seconds in ZL1 variant.

Handling

Magnetic ride control ensures precision cornering and stability.

Aerodynamics

Streamlined design improves downforce and reduces drag at high speeds.

Braking

Brembo high-performance brakes provide powerful and safe stopping.
